I created this atlas as my final project for a Graphic Design in Cartography class during my second semester as a GIS and Cartography certificate student. Through I not only wanted to explore the geographic patterns of smoking rates in the United States, but I also wanted to utilize a unique mapping technique that allows us to communicate two variables. The techniques used are known as bivariate choropleth and value by alpha. Each can be difficult to interpret at times, but have the ability to reveal very interesting patterns.
